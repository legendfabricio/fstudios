KAYPA

Kaypa is a mapping web-series uploaded on Youtube. It consists of a world of fictional countries which war can happen at any time

To know more about Kaypa, visit the Kaypapedia:

Kaypan Civil War (Episode 1)

Birds are singing, flowers are blooming, as the world is in peace. However, some people does not like the current Kaypa government, and these are the socialists.

So, they plan to invade Kaypa and cause a civil war. But how will they grow their party, you may ask? Well, with propagandas, of course.

With enough propagandas and people, they finally decide to invade Kaypa by the province of Latipa, and they kind of did a good job

But they were not the only ones who wanted the power of the government: there was the French Rebels.

The French Rebels also revolted against the government, in the province of Guhai. but since they were weak, they were short-lived

The Socialists fails to revolt, and some time later the French Rebels also falls, making the war end.

Kaypa 1 was made in December 11, 2022. The total frames was less than 1mb.

Kaypan Civil War (Episode 2)

A month later the Kaypan Civil War ended, with the Kaypan victory, the Socialists still didn't gave up.

The Socialists did a lot more of propaganda to expand their thing and tried to be stronger in military than Kaypa.

The war starts, with the Socialists having a stronger military. They already take alot of land.

French Kaypa, hoping to get their independence again, gets advantage of the second civil war and revolts too.

Carnicanus helps Kaypa by sending to them resources for the war

But then, Anuix interferes in the war to gain territory, and actually does it.

Carnicanus quits sending resources and helps Kaypa

French Kaypa gets their independence as they wanted, but that would not go for too long.

Anuix declares war at French Kaypa and invades through all direction. and so French Kaypa turns into Anuixian Kaypa.

Guhai and some territory were lost, and the Socialists won, declaring the loss of the Kaypan government.

Kaypa 2 was made in 12 January, 2023. The total frames was 1mb (25 frames)
